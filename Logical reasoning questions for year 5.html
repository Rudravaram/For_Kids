Math Logical Questions for Year 5

Question 1:

If you have 3 apples and you take away 2, how many do you have?

Question 2:

What is the next number in the sequence: 2, 4, 6, 8, ...?

Question 3:

Tom has twice as many marbles as Jerry. If Jerry has 5 marbles, how many marbles does Tom have?

Question 4:

There are 10 birds on a tree. If 3 fly away, how many are left?

Question 5:

What is the sum of the angles in a triangle?
